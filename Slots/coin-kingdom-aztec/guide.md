# Coin Kingdom Aztec

## 🟢 When to play

This is a **dual must-hit-by** Ainsworth title: **Grand $10,000** (resets ~$8,000) and **Major $1,000** (resets ~$800). Same math on **Coin Kingdom Aztec** and **Coin Kingdom Egyptian** ... pick whichever skin is on the floor.

**Grand chase:** Use the **MHB calculator** with **$10,000** cap, **~$8,000** reset, and **~$6.67 meter rise per $1,000 coin-in** (verify on your bank). For base-game RTP in the calc, **subtract ~2%** from the paytable RTP you believe you are on ... the progressive contribution is not in that headline number.

**Major-only or combo:** Major runs about **3.5×** the Grand meter speed. You can enter Grand earlier when Major is high and factor expected Major hits during the chase into your Grand EV (each ~$70 you expect to add on the Grand path pulls Major up ~$245).

Rough **Major breakeven** (hits at ceiling ~$990, not random early):

| 89-90% | ~$965-968+ |
| 91% | ~$963+ |

Grand needs to be **much closer to ceiling** (~$9,900+) for most RTPs ... run your numbers; **taxes on a $10k handpay** matter if you are grinding Grand.

**Denom tip:** Prefer **higher denom, lower bet within that denom** when available. Fixed Mini/Minor scale with denom, not bet ... winning a $10 Mini on a $7.50 bet hurts.

## 🛑 When to stop

**Major:** This ladder **almost always pays at ~$990** (sometimes a few cents over). It *can* pop early ... do not assume it will not ... but once you are in range, plan on riding to the ceiling. If you need a **card pull with a shown loss**, spin off **before** it crosses ~$990.

**Grand:** Field reports cluster near **~$9,990** (one documented hit at $9,990.02). Treat it like a ceiling game unless you have evidence otherwise.

**Mystery Match tell:** Jackpots **only** pay in the **Mystery Match 3** pick feature (predetermined ... your taps do not matter). If you trigger Mystery Match **right as Major crosses ~$990**, you are getting Major.

Stop when your ladder hits or you exhaust session bankroll. Do not max-bet a $1,000 MHB because you are close ... variance scales hard with bet.

## 🔍 How to check (quick/easy)

1. **Confirm both MHB labels** on the glass (Grand $10k / Major $1k). Class II copies without MHB exist ... walk away.
2. **Photo both meters**, denom, bet, and any RTP/paytable sticker.
3. **Major meter quirk:** Display may read like a **$3.00** meter but true seed is **~$2.85**. At **$3/spin** it usually ticks **$0.01**; every **~19 spins** you will see **$0.02** to true up the extra **$0.15** per cycle. Do not judge the meter off $30 coin-in ... spin enough at your intended bet to see the double-tick.
4. **Fake feature hype:** Three discs above the reels **do not** gate bonuses ... features can trigger anytime. Ignore them for AP decisions.

## ⚠️ Risk & Warnings

**Bankroll on hand: 300-500+ units** at min qualifying bet (session length and how early you enter drive this hard)

**Extremely volatile base game.** You can bleed fast in dead spins; recovery depends on **100×-200×+** feature hits and the occasional monster line (especially **stacked Multiplier Wilds + Expanded Ways + Royals Removed**).

- **Multiplier Wilds** = highest variance (feast or famine).
- **Royals Removed** = steadiest features, usually respectable but not life-changing.
- **Expanded Ways** = lots of retriggers, often small per-spin pay unless **Coin Kingdom** symbols line up deep.

**Bet minimum** that still fits your time budget. MHB at max bet can dump **thousands** on a bad run; min bet = fewer W-2Gs, smoother path, less gambling between +EV ladder points. Calculator **Time required** block: this game is slow ... use **~1,700 spins/hour**.

**Combination play** is advanced ... only stack Major EV into Grand if you are comfortable with dual-ladder bookkeeping.

## 🎰 Gameplay Mechanics

**Coin Kingdom** (Aztec / Egyptian) is a **5×4**, **1,024-ways** (up to **3,456** in some bonuses) Ainsworth slot built around **three feature guardians** and a **Mystery Match 3** jackpot picker.

**Base layout:** Wins left-to-right on adjacent reels. **Wilds** land **reels 2-4 only**. **Coin Kingdom** emblem is top pay and pays from **two-of-a-kind**; royals (A-10) are filler.

**Free-game triggers (8 spins to start):** Each mode is tied to a symbol on a fixed reel and can fire **randomly** when that symbol lands ... the sliding discs above the reels are **cosmetic**, not timing gates.

| Feature | Trigger reel | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Multiplier Wilds** | Queen - reel 1 | Wilds carry **×2-×5** on wins they complete |
| **Expanded Ways** | King - reel 3 | Reels 2-4 grow to **6 high** → up to **3,456 ways** |
| **Royals Removed** | Warrior - reel 5 | **10-A removed**; premiums + wilds only |

**Retriggers:** **2-5 Coin symbols** during features award **3 / 8 / 15 / 20** extra games (same scale in each mode).

**Combined features:** Land **two or three** guardians together and you can get **stacked** mechanics in one bonus ... that is where the huge line hits live.

**Mystery Match 3:** Can trigger on a paid spin when **no** guardian landed. Pick from **20 coins** until **three match** ... outcome is **predetermined**. Awards bet-multiplied credits or a **Mini / Minor / Major / Grand** tier. **Major and Grand MHB jackpots pay only here**, not on random line hits.

**Fixed jackpots (online reference ... verify your denom/bet on floor):** Mini **12.5×**, Minor **62.5×**, Major **312.5×**, Grand **625×** total bet at listed online config; live Vegas MHB amounts are the **$1k / $10k** ladders above, not these multiples alone.

**Premium symbols (Aztec skin):** Coin Kingdom logo, jaguar, eagle, knife ... royals pay crumbs. Egyptian skin swaps art; pay structure is the same family.
