# Phoenix Link

## 🟢 When to play

Look for **high Phoenix counters**, adjusted for bet level and denomination. 

Use your Phoenix Link calculator for precise take points. Lower bets on low denoms (88% RTP range) usually need **high 1400s**, while higher bets on higher denoms (92-93% RTP) can be playable in the **high 1200s**.

![Phoenix Link counter: free feature must be won before 1888 collected](/guides/phoenix-link/counter.webp)



## 🛑 When to stop

Play until the bonus triggers or the counter drops significantly below your calculated **+EV threshold**. Do not chase **low counters** on **poor RTP setups**.

## 🔍 How to check (Pain in the ass) 

The **Phoenix counter** is **clearly visible** in the top right. Always cycle through **every denomination and bet level** (counters are bet-specific). Insert a **checker ticket** (smaller than lowest bet) to cycle through all bets and denoms. 

**TIP:** *To save time when cycling through bets, on the hand pad there are two "Lines" options, the lowest lines option is selected by default. On lowest lines, check bets **3, 4, & 5**, then change to higher lines option and check bets **2, 3, & 5**. This will save you time by not checking the same bet multiple times across lines options.*

## ⚠️ Risk & Warnings

**Bankroll on hand: 400 units** (depends heavily on bet size and target counter)

This is a **HIGHLY VOLATILE** machine. RTP varies significantly by bet/denom - just a couple % points in RTP drastically affect counter take points. Even at +EV thresholds you can still have rough sessions if you dont hit bonuses along the way or they pay poorly. It is not uncommon to lose 300 units even on counters in the 1400 range. 

Due to the highly volatile nature of this game, I included a **Walk Away Advisor** in the calculator to help gauge optimal stopping points where you can consider walking away or selling off a % - even when the machine remains in a **+EV state**. Yes, +EV is +EV, but run a hundred of these plays and I guarantee you'll start considering it!

## 🎰 Gameplay Mechanics

**Phoenix Link** is a **5-reel, up to 100-line** (or ways) video slot by Aristocrat (released in 2024) built around a **Must-Hit-By Phoenix Counter** that drives the advantage play opportunity.

Every **Phoenix symbol** that lands (in base game or during bonuses) increments the visible counter by 1. The counter has a **must-hit-by of 1888**, but it can trigger randomly at any time before then. When it triggers, you enter a **Hold & Spin** feature where Phoenix symbols lock in place with credit values, and/or multiple Mini/Minor Bonuses, multiple Major Jackpots, and of course the ever-elusive Grand Jackpot.

The counter is **fully persistent and bet-specific** - each denomination and bet level has its own independent counter. This is why checking every bet level is critical.

Additional features include:
- **Hold & Spin** with chances for Mini/Minor/Major/Grand jackpots (Mini/Minor Bonuses and Major Jackpot scale by denom)
- **Free Games** with Twin Spins or 2x Wild multipliers (depending on theme)
- Phoenix symbols continue to increment the counter even during the bonus

The combination of a visible **must-hit counter** + massive **RTP variance** across bet levels makes this one of the more complex but rewarding AP games when you use proper **take points**.
